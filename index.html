Very fresh! 🌿

• Eggs are collected daily
• Graded and packed right away
• You receive them within 24-48 hours of collection
• Never stored for long periods
• Always date-stamped for transparency
Shelf Life:
• At room temperature: 7-10 days
• Refrigerated: 3-4 weeks

Storage Tips:
• Keep eggs in a cool, dry place
• Best stored in the fridge with the pointed end down to keep the yolk centered
• Avoid washing until ready to cook (preserves natural protective coating)
• Store away from strong-smelling foods

💡 For best taste and safety, we recommend refrigeration!

✨ Did You Know?

🥚

The freshest eggs will sink in water, while older eggs float — a simple kitchen test you can do at home! 💧

💡

Storing eggs with the pointed end down keeps the yolk centered and helps them stay fresh longer. ❄️

✨

Smaller eggs often have a higher yolk-to-white ratio, meaning more of certain nutrients per gram!
